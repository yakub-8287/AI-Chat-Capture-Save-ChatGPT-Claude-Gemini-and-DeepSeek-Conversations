FAQ
Common questions
Is my data private and secure?+
Yes. All your AI conversations are stored locally on your device using Chrome's built-in storage. Nothing is uploaded to any server. Your data never leaves your browser without your explicit consent.
Does it work with free ChatGPT (no Plus)?+
Yes. AI Chat Capture works with any version of ChatGPT — free, Plus, or Team. You don't need a paid ChatGPT subscription to use this extension.
What happens to my data if I uninstall?+
Locally stored conversations are removed when you uninstall. Before uninstalling, export all your saved ideas as a JSON file from the extension popup — it takes one click.
Can I cancel my Pro subscription anytime?+
Yes, anytime. After cancellation, you keep Pro access until the end of your billing period, then revert to Free. No questions asked.
Which AI platforms are supported?+
Free plan: ChatGPT and Google Gemini. Pro plan: ChatGPT, Google Gemini, Claude (claude.ai), Perplexity AI, Grok (grok.com), and DeepSeek (chat.deepseek.com) — all 6 supported platforms.
Is there a refund policy?+
Yes. We offer a 7-day refund from the date of first payment if you're not satisfied. Contact us at gajjarjay2023@gmail.com and we'll process it promptly.
How do I save and download all my AI conversations?+
Open the AI Chat Capture extension popup and click the Export button. Choose JSON (for re-importing later) or PDF / Print — opens a print-ready page with all saved conversations. Use Chrome's "Save as PDF" to download as a PDF file.
How to save ChatGPT conversation as PDF (2026)?+
Install AI Chat Capture → chat on ChatGPT normally → extension auto-saves conversations → click Export → PDF/Print → Chrome opens a formatted page → press Ctrl+P (Cmd+P on Mac) → Save as PDF. All your ChatGPT conversations saved as a clean, organized PDF.
